# 55 - Alias Creation Rules

## Global requirements

- Aliases must always define 5-9 high value, verified tags from our tag list.
- Do not include unverified or ad-hoc tags.
- Avoid overlap with other alias categories. If a tag belongs to another category, remove it.

## Character alias rules

- Character aliases must only define outfit, appearance, and accessories.
- Character aliases must never define gender, scene, or emotion.

## Category-specific rules

### Camera

- Focus on framing, lens, composition, perspective, and motion.
- Do not include scene/location, time/weather, or emotion/mood tags.

### Scene

- Focus on location, environment, and setting details.
- Do not include camera/composition, lighting, character appearance, or emotion tags.

### Lighting

- Focus on light quality, direction, and source.
- Do not include composition, location, or emotion tags.

### Weather

- Focus on atmospheric conditions and sky state.
- Do not include lighting setup, camera/composition, or emotion tags.

### Pose

- Focus on body posture and action.
- Do not include emotion/mood, camera/composition, or scene tags.

### Mood/Emotion

- Focus on facial expression and affect.
- Do not include camera/composition, location, or pose/action tags.

### Outfit

- Focus on clothing and wardrobe styling.
- Do not include body/appearance, pose, scene, or emotion tags.

### Hair/Appearance

- Focus on hair and appearance details.
- Do not include outfit, pose, scene, or emotion tags.

### Props/Accessories

- Focus on handhelds, wearables, and small objects.
- Do not include outfit, pose, scene, or emotion tags.
